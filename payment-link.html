Get Started

Private web3 payments made easy

Create a zkBob private payment link and share it with ANYONE, ANYWHERE (email, telegram, discord etc). They click the link, connect any wallet, choose any token, and press send. That’s it! The sender doesn’t need a zkBob account, and you get paid quickly, with low fees and 100% privacy.

Advantages

Receive P2P funds from anyone, anytime

Share a link to receive tokens privately from different people or groups in chats, forums, Discord, Twitter, email and more. Easy to share, and easy to send funds!

Send popular tokens, discreetly

Select ETH, DAI, USDC or other popular tokens when sending. Tokens are converted under the hood with low conversion fees, and the receiver gets tokens deposited directly into their zkAccount.

Personal privacy protection

Protect sensitive information like the total amount of assets and NFTs you hold in your digital wallet, the amount you pay for goods or services, and funding senders and recipients.

Simple for the sender

The sender doesn’t need a zkAccount. All they need is a web3 wallet and tokens of their choice on the appropriate network to send you a private payment.
